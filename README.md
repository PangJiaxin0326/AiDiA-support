## FAQ

**Q: Why do I need to enter my own API key?**
AiDiA uses your AI provider directly — Anthropic, OpenAI, or
local Ollama — so your entries never pass through our servers.

**Q: Where is my journal data stored?**
On your device, and synced to your personal iCloud account.
No third-party servers, no analytics.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
Email me with your iOS version and a description of what happened.
