Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to enter my own API key?

AiDiA uses your chosen AI provider directly — Anthropic, OpenAI, or a local Ollama server — so your journal entries never pass through our servers.

Where is my journal data stored?

On your device, and optionally synced to your personal iCloud account through Apple's CloudKit. No third-party servers, no analytics.

How do I report a bug?

Email me with your iOS version and a short description of what happened. Screenshots help.

How do I delete my data?

Delete the app from your device. If iCloud sync was enabled, also remove the AiDiA container from Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Storage.
